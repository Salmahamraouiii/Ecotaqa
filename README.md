
# ⚡ EcoTaqa
**Smart Energy Monitoring & Sustainability Platform**

Eco Taqa is a smart web-based platform designed to **monitor, analyze, and optimize electricity consumption** for both individuals and organizations.  
It provides **real-time insights, regional visualization, alerts, and sustainability tracking** to reduce energy waste and improve efficiency.

---

## 🌍 Project Vision

Electricity waste often occurs due to:
- Lack of real-time consumption visibility
- Poor detection of abnormal or peak usage
- Limited awareness of energy costs and environmental impact

### Our Solution
Eco Taqa acts as a **digital energy companion** that:
- Makes energy usage visible and understandable
- Detects waste and inefficiencies
- Encourages responsible and sustainable energy behavior
- Supports companies in tracking eco-friendly goals

---

## 🧱 Platform Overview

Eco Taqa is a **web platform** composed of dashboards and monitoring tools connected to a centralized backend.

| Component | Purpose | Target Users |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| 💻 Web Application | Monitoring & analytics | Users, Companies |
| 🛠️ Admin Panel | System & data management | Administrators |

## 💻 Key Features

### 👤 User & Company Accounts
- Secure authentication for users and companies  
- Separate dashboards for personal and organizational energy data  

---

### ⚡ Real-Time Energy Monitoring
- Live electricity consumption tracking  
- Instant visibility of spikes and waste  

---

### 📈 Historical Consumption Data
- Daily, weekly, and monthly usage history  
- Trend analysis to track improvements over time  

---

### 🗺️ Regional Energy Map
- Visual map showing energy usage by region  
- Color-coded indicators:
  - 🔴 High usage
  - 🟡 Medium usage
  - 🟢 Low usage  

---

### 🏢 Building & Department Monitoring
- Energy usage by:
  - Building
  - Floor
  - Department  
- Helps organizations identify inefficient areas  

---

### ⏱️ Peak Consumption Detection
- Identifies hours of highest electricity usage  
- Supports load balancing and peak reduction  

---

### 🚨 Alerts & Notifications
- Real-time alerts when consumption exceeds thresholds  
- Helps prevent excessive or abnormal usage  

---

### 🔍 Energy Waste Detection
- Detects unusual or excessive consumption patterns  
- Highlights potential energy leaks or misuse  

---

### 💡 Personalized Energy-Saving Tips
- Simple recommendations based on usage behavior  
- Tailored for users and companies  

---

### 📊 Energy Efficiency Score
- Each user or company receives an efficiency score  
- Encourages improvement through measurable performance  

---

### 💰 Cost Estimation
- Estimated electricity costs based on consumption  
- Shows financial impact of energy waste  

---

### 🗓️ Weekly & Monthly Reports
- Automated reports summarizing:
  - Consumption
  - Waste
  - Improvements  
- Easy performance tracking over time  

---

### 🎮 Gamification & Rewards
- Points or badges for reducing energy usage  
- Motivates sustainable behavior  

---

### 📚 Educational Content
- Tips and mini-lessons on energy saving  
- Raises awareness and promotes best practices  

---

### 🌱 Sustainability Tracking
- Tracks progress toward eco-friendly goals  
- Supports long-term sustainability strategies
